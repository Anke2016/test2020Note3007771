U1 &CAUSE&                                                                 
AS The system detected that certain functions in this system are not       
   activated.                                                              
U1 &SYSTEM_RESPONSE&                                                       
AS Displays the packages that were not deployed, since the functions of the
   switch in question are deactivated in Switch Framework.                 
AS For each deactivated switch, the associated ABAP packaged are displayed 
   in parentheses.                                                         
U1 &WHAT_TO_DO&                                                            
AS No action is required. The packages are deployed to the SAP HANA        
   repository automatically as soon as the functions are enabled in Switch 
   Framework.                                                              
AS <ZH>Note</>: Functions in Switch Framework can be enabled using         
   transaction SFW5 or by importing switch entries.
